Why RheumAuth

Built for the real medication-access problems rheumatology clinics face.

The hardest part is not just writing a letter. It is knowing what the payer wants, what is missing from the note, and what to do when the first submission fails.

New prior auths

Turn clinic notes into payer-aware authorization drafts with clear missing-info detection before submission.

Denial analysis

Classify denial language so staff know whether to appeal, resubmit, switch, or escalate.

Renewals

Generate continuation summaries that show benefit, flare prevention, and interruption risk.

Peer-to-peer prep

Give physicians a concise case summary and sharper approval talking points before insurer calls.

How it works

A simple workflow your staff can use in minutes.

Keep the experience fast, quiet, and clinically grounded.

1. Select the case context

Choose diagnosis, drug, payer, plan, and route so the system knows the likely coverage pathway.

2. Paste the note or denial

The engine extracts treatment failures, screening, disease activity, and contraindications.

3. Generate the best next step

Create a prior auth, appeal, peer-to-peer sheet, or renewal summary with visible missing-info warnings.
