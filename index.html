      About RheumAuth
      RheumAuth is a clinical decision support tool for rheumatology practices. It helps prepare prior authorization requests, appeal denials, and prepare for peer-to-peer reviews using AI.

      Disclaimer: RheumAuth is a support tool. All generated content should be reviewed by a licensed physician before submission. Insurance requirements change frequently — always verify with the payer directly.
