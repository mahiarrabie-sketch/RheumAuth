How it works

Simple enough for a new staff member to understand in one sitting

The interface is built around a short path from note to action, with large sections, plain labels, and clear next steps.
1

Choose the case

Pick the diagnosis, medication, payer, and plan so the platform understands the context.

2

Paste the note or denial

RheumAuth reads the note and highlights what was found and what still needs attention.

3

Generate the next step

Create a prior auth draft, appeal, renewal summary, or peer-to-peer prep sheet.

Clear and trustworthy by design
