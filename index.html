Interface philosophy

Simple enough for a busy clinic to actually use

Large text, plain labels, clear status colors, and obvious next steps. The design is intentionally calm so older physicians and staff can move quickly without fighting the software.
1. Choose the case context
Select the payer, plan, drug, diagnosis, and route.
2. Review what was found
See green, yellow, and red signals for what is present, weak, or missing.
3. Generate the next move
Create the PA, appeal, renewal summary, or peer-to-peer sheet.
